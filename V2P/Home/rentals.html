V2P Rentals

Our Products

Payments are not driven by market dynamics and have to be made irrespective of the business cycle. Moreover rentals are contractual advance payments fragmented and spread across several rental points. Rentals are not made on any one particular day leading to a cumbersome reconciliation process, manual interventions and several diversions.


V2p platform facilitates rental payments; a category where there is no credit and largely advance payments. Our solution automates and simplifies the rental payment process by adding value to both; the receiver and payer of the rent.
