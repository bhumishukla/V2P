About us

Vendor to pay has emanated out of years of experience in dealing with Community and Cooperative Development, Payment, Banking processes and Financial services. Given the deep understanding of the Industry landscape it has evolved as an extension of existing business processes.


V2p is a comprehensive, flexible and a cloud-based solution with core focus of vendor management through streamlining supply chain metrics and generating multiple benefits.


V2p offers a complete and seamless integration from the buyers back end system to final reconciliations.


V2p has forged strong partnerships with payment companies, e-procurement vendors, and integration specialists.
