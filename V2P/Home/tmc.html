Our Products

Travel management companies undergo a gross mismatch between current assets and liabilities on the other hand online portals have led to consolidation of the market in the hands of a few leading to competitive advantages.  Billing and settlement plans (BSP) designed to facilitate and simplify the selling, reporting and remitting procedures for Sales agents worldwide are posing new constraints in a market where the norm is to avail a monthly credit line. In the domestic travel market Low cost carriers work on a cash down model increasing pressure on working capital, imbalance between inflow and outflow of funds for Sales agents and intermediaries.


V2p platform is created to provide an end to end solution to Travel Management Companies, where in they can meet their commitments to Airlines, Hotels and Ground Transportation providers. V2p proposition creates an inflow of funds through multiple service providers to the required levels in your working capital optimising rebate programs and lower financing costs.
